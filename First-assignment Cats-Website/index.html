My website is about .... Cats!

Domestication

The earliest evidence for the taming of cats was excavated near a human grave in Cyprus, dating to about 7500–7200 BCE.

In fact, the 8-month-old cat was buried with its human owner, which show that cats were not just kept as pest controllers (to keep rodents at bay).

Spread

The domestic cat is a species that can be found almost everywhere where human populations have settled.

Due to its ability to thrive in almost any terrestrial habitat, it is among the world's most invasive species.

Culture & Religion

Cats had several important roles in religious beliefs as well, often distinctly present in cultures around the world.

In Ancient Egypt, a feline-like goddess, called Bastet, was the defender of the king, and the sun god, Ra. She was also responsible of the fertility of women. Their cultural depiction is equally old. Cats are featured in the history of many nations, are the subject of legend, and are a favorite subject of artists and writers.

Cats & Arts

Evidently, as cats play such an important role in many cultures, artists frequently draw their inspiration from cats too.
